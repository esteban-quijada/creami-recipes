## Strawberry Protein Ice Cream

**Base type:** Lite Ice Cream

This recipe yields a delicious Strawberry Protein Ice Cream with 250 calories and 23g of protein.

### Ingredients

- 340g (11.5 fl oz / 1 bottle) Fairlife Strawberry Milk (or Strawberry Protein Shake)
- 10-15g sweetener
- 1g (¼ tsp) salt
- 1g (¼ tsp) xanthan gum

### Steps

1.  Blend 340g (11.5 fl oz / 1 bottle) Fairlife Strawberry Milk (or Strawberry Protein Shake), 10-15g sweetener, 1g (¼ tsp) salt, and 1g (¼ tsp) xanthan gum for the base.
2.  Freeze the blended mixture for at least 16 hours in a Ninja Creami pint container.
3.  Before processing, run the frozen pint under hot water for at least 60 seconds.
4.  Spin the pint on the "Lite Ice Cream" setting.
5.  Spin the pint again on the "Respin" setting.
6.  Respin again if the texture is powdery after the second spin.

### Notes

- This recipe contains approximately 250 calories, 21g carbohydrates, 8g fat, and 23g protein.
