## Peanut Butter Salted Caramel Protein Ice Cream

**Base type:** Lite Ice Cream

This recipe creates a high-protein, low-calorie peanut butter salted caramel ice cream featuring a Barebells protein bar, yielding 465 calories and 45g protein.

### Ingredients

- 400g (about 1⅔ cups) Fat Free Fairlife Milk
- 40-45g (about 3⅓ to 3¾ tbsp) 1:1 sugar substitute
- 8g (1 tbsp) PB powder (like PB Fit or PB2)
- 2g (½ tsp) salt
- 1g (¼ tsp) xanthan gum
- 1 count Soft Barebells Peanut Caramel Protein Bar (for mix-ins)

### Steps

1.  Add 400g (about 1⅔ cups) Fat Free Fairlife Milk, 40-45g (about 3⅓ to 3¾ tbsp) 1:1 sugar substitute, 8g (1 tbsp) PB powder, 2g (½ tsp) salt, and 1g (¼ tsp) xanthan gum to a blender. Blend all ingredients for the base.
2.  Pour the mixture into a Ninja Creami pint container and freeze for at least 16 hours.
3.  Before processing, run the frozen pint under hot water for at least 60 seconds.
4.  Process on the "Lite Ice Cream" setting.
5.  Process again on the "Respin" setting.
6.  If the texture is still powdery after the respin, re-spin again.
7.  Make a hole down to the bottom of the pint with a butter knife and fill with 1 Soft Barebells Peanut Caramel Protein Bar.
8.  Process on the "Mix-in" cycle.
