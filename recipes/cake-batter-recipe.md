## Cake Batter Protein Ice Cream

**Base type:** Protein Ice Cream

135 calories | 22g protein (without mix-ins) 270 calories | 24g protein (with mix-ins).

### Ingredients

- 400g (about 1⅔ cups) Fat Free Fairlife Milk
- 45g (about 3½ tbsp) sweetener
- 5g (1 tsp) cake batter extract
- 1g (¼ tsp) salt
- 1g (¼ tsp) xanthan gum
- 35g Cake Pieces or Mini Muffins (for mix-ins)

### Steps

1. Blend 400g (about 1⅔ cups) Fat Free Fairlife Milk, 45g (about 3½ tbsp) sweetener, 5g (1 tsp) cake batter extract, 1g (¼ tsp) salt, and 1g (¼ tsp) xanthan gum for the base.
2. Pour the mixture into a Ninja Creami pint container and freeze for at least 16 hours.
3. Remove from the freezer and spin on the “Lite Ice Cream” setting.
4. Spin again on the “Respin” setting.
5. Respin again if the texture is powdery.
6. Make a hole down to the bottom of the pint with a butter knife and fill with 35g Cake Pieces or Mini Muffins.
7. Run the “Mix-in” cycle.
